About Us

Founded by five friends with a shared passion for environmental sustainability

Trach Cash was born from a simple idea: that waste isn't just a problem, but an opportunity. Founded by five friends who shared a vision of a cleaner planet, we've grown into a company that transforms environmental challenges into sustainable solutions.

Our mission is to revolutionize waste management through innovative recycling processes, education, and community engagement. We believe that small changes can make a big difference, and we're committed to being the change we want to see in the world.
